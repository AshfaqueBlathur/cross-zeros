how to play?

  • Hatch me is an offline dual tactics game. Board will be having 36 eggs, which we need to hatch through playing.
  • Both the players should come in to a desicion, wether they are blue one or the red one.
  • Game will start with a toss to determain who makes the first move. (If any of you fails, don't care. that's not a big deal.)
  • When a player makes a move, the next chance will always be for the other player.
  • Whenever any coloum or row fills with all omlets by any moves, the player who made that move will be rewarded with the respective number of omlets in the coloumn/row.
  • While all the valuble eggs are made omlet, the game ends!

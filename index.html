Cross zeros is an ancient pen and paper game. Usually played by two or more players. (for the sake of some technical issues this game currently only supports 1 v/s 1 onlie)

  • How To Play!
  • game will start as soon as one of you connects
  • first turn is absolutly randomly selected
  • you should wait until the opponent makes his move (you may talk to the opponent using the chat fecility provided for ensuring his presence in game)
  • on crossing a row or coloumn completely the one making the last move will be granded with the length of respective row or coloumn.
  • on crossing all the zeros present, the game will declare its winner by means of score.
  • you should ensure a relible connection otherwise the opponent will be won without any further notice :)
  • the one who wins will get the first turn on restarting the game!
